[h1]🎵 核心机制介绍[/h1]
[b]MJ巡演舞台模式[/b]
当你在野外被一定数量的丧尸包围时，有概率触发惊悚舞台事件！在你遇到危险时，MJ将与他的伴舞团一起向你靠近！
这到底是生机……还是危机……？取决于你的应对方式！
[list]
[*] [b]多曲目巡演与落幕：[/b] 经典曲目《Thriller》、《Beat It》、《Smooth Criminal》轮番轰炸！
[*] [b]安可加演：[/b] 只要你成功在混乱中活了下来，并且确保了 MJ 与他的所有伴舞的存活，在演出结束时有概率触发[b]安可舞台[/b]！
[*] [b]护航机制与反击：[/b] MJ收到攻击可能会[b]瞬移避让[/b]（但也可能因某些神秘原因闪避失败，明明代码都对了，但莫名就时好时坏），并指挥舞团全员对你进行热烈反击！只要场上还有伴舞存活，MJ 就接近不死！甚至，这些伴舞在MJ存活期间还会持续回血！太刺激咧！
[*] [b]和平观演奖励：[/b] 只要你“老老实实”在舞台演出范围内看完整场演出且不伤害 MJ与伴舞们，他们"谢幕离开"时，你会获得[b]特殊的谢幕礼包[/b]！
[*] [b]粉丝暴动：[/b] 如果你在中途杀死了 MJ，将彻底激怒周围的所有粉丝！所有粉丝们都获得了强化😡请接受粉丝之怒的洗礼吧老伙计们！！
[/list]

[h1]🎵 Core Features[/h1]

[b]MJ World Tour Stage Mode[/b]
When you get swarmed by a horde out in the wild, there's a chance to trigger the Thriller Stage event! Right in your moment of peril, MJ and his backup dancers will slide up on you!
Is this your salvation... or your doom...? That depends entirely on how you play it!

[list]
[*] [b]Multi-Track Tour & Curtain Call:[/b] Iconic hits like *Thriller*, *Beat It*, and *Smooth Criminal* blasting back-to-back!
[*] [b]Encore Performance:[/b] If you manage to survive the chaotic dance party *and* keep MJ and all his backup dancers alive, there's a chance to trigger an [b]Encore[/b] at the end of the show!
[*] [b]VIP Escort & Counterattack:[/b] Taking damage might cause MJ to [b]teleport out of harm's way[/b] (though sometimes the flash dodge fails for mysterious reasons—the code looks totally right, but it's just finicky like that). He’ll also order his troupe to go full aggro on you! As long as there’s at least one backup dancer standing, MJ is practically immortal! Plus, the dancers passively regenerate health while MJ is alive. Pure, unadulterated chaos!
[*] [b]Passivist Audience Rewards:[/b] If you play nice, stay within the stage boundary, and watch the full performance without hitting MJ or his dancers, they’ll drop a [b]Special Farewell Gift Bag[/b] during their curtain call!
[*] [b]Fan Riot:[/b] If you kill MJ mid-show, you will enraged his fanbase! The crowd goes berserk with massive buffs 😡 Prepare to feel the wrath of the stans, my friends!!
[/list]

Pro tip: Trigger chances double at night. After all—
*Cause' this is Thriller! Thriller Night!*
